Fresh fruits and vegetables are an important part of a healthy diet.
They contain essential vitamins, minerals, fiber and other nutrients
that are essential for good health. In fact, research has shown
that a healthy diet rich in fruits and vegetables may reduce the risk
of cancer and other chronic diseases.
